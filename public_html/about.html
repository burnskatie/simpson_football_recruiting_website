All Americans

Throughout history of Simpson College Football, there has been a total of 22 All-Americans in D3 football from Simpson. In the future, we hope to add more with the talented student athletes from our program.

Academic Excellence

In the last two years at Simpson College, there has been 27 Academic All-Conference players who have recieved this reward. We look for players who not only succeed on the field but also in the classroom.

Why Simpson?

  • Great Location
    • Simpson is located just south of Des Moines. Both Indianola and Des Moines offer plenty of places to eat and fun activities.
  • Campus Life
    • Simpson has a tight knit campus community and there is always something to do right here, on campus
  • Storm Athletics
    • Obviously football is the reason you are viewing this page, but the athletics department runs deep with many sports represented. Watching other Storm teams compete at a high level is another advantage of coming to Simpson College.
